Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority.

Eligibility for Return

We accept returns for products within 7 days of delivery, provided the item is unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as delivered. Certain products such as opened consumables or used healthcare devices may not be eligible for return due to hygiene and safety reasons.

Non-returnable Items

The following categories are non-returnable unless damaged or incorrect:

  • Opened medicine strips
  • Personal hygiene products
  • Temperature-sensitive items (e.g. insulin, vaccines)
  • Healthcare devices that have been unsealed or used

How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please contact our support team via email at support@healthcareplus.in or call our helpline. Provide your order ID, product details, and reason for return. Once approved, our logistics team will arrange pickup or provide drop-off instructions.
