Philosophy:

Dr. K.K. Aggarwal's philosophy on health and medicine was deeply rooted in a holistic and public-centric approach, often blending modern medical science with traditional Indian wisdom. Key aspects of his philosophy include: "One Health" Concept: He strongly believed in the interconnectedness of human health with animal health, plant health, and the environment. This reflects the ancient Vedic saying, "VasudhaivaKutumbakam" (the whole world is my family), and "Yathapindetathabrahmande,yathabrahmandetathapinde" (as is the microcosm, so is the macrocosm). He emphasized that the human body is a replica of the universe and that wellness should prioritize this interconnectedness.
